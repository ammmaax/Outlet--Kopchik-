Returns & Refunds

At COPCHIK, customer satisfaction is important to us. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may request a return according to the conditions outlined below.

Return Period

Items can be returned within 14 days of receiving the order. Returned products must be in their original condition, unworn, unwashed, and with all tags attached.

Eligible Items

Clothing, footwear, and accessories may be returned if they have not been used and remain in their original packaging.

Non-Returnable Items

Products that show signs of wear, damage caused by the customer, or items without original tags may not be eligible for return.

Refund Process

Once the returned item has been received and inspected, a refund will be issued to the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

Exchange Policy

If you would like a different size or product, we recommend returning the original item and placing a new order.

Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item received was incorrect or defective.
