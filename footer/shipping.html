Shipping Information

At COPCHIK, we aim to provide a smooth and reliable shipping experience for all visitors and customers. We work to ensure that every order is processed quickly and delivered safely.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days after confirmation. During periods of high demand, processing may take slightly longer.

Domestic Shipping

Deliveries within Ukraine usually arrive within 2–5 business days depending on the destination and shipping method selected.

International Shipping

International orders are delivered to most countries worldwide. Delivery times generally range from 7–21 business days depending on the destination country and customs procedures.

Shipping Costs

Shipping fees are calculated based on the destination and package size. Final shipping costs will be displayed during checkout before payment is completed.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number that allows you to monitor the delivery status of your package.

Important Notice

Delivery times may vary due to weather conditions, customs inspections, public holidays, or other factors beyond our control.

If you have any questions regarding shipping or delivery, please contact our support team. We will be happy to assist you.
